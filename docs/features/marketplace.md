# Marketplace (Buy & Sell)

The marketplace is the primary AI-driven experience. It supports natural-language discovery and
matching across business categories.

## Capabilities
- Product and service search
- Vendor discovery and recommendations
- Category-specific workflows (pharmacy, hardware, groceries, services)

## Constraints
- Rwanda (RW) only
- Currency: RWF
- UI languages: English and French

## Entry Point
WhatsApp messages are routed to the Buy & Sell agent via `wa-webhook-buy-sell` and shared tooling.
